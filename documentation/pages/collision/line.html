collision.line

collision.line(mask,mx,my,angle,x1,y1,x2,y2);

mask: [array] The mask to check for a collision.

mx: [number] The position of the mask horizontally within space.

my: [number] The position of the mask vertically within space.

angle: [number] Rotation of the mask.

x1: [number] The horizontal component of the first point of the line.

y1: [number] The vertical component of the first point of the line.

x2: [number] The horizontal component of the second point of the line.

y2: [number] The vertical component of the second point of the line.
