collision.masks

collision.masks(mask1,x1,y1,angle1,mask2,x2,y2,angle2);

mask1: [array] The first mask to check if it collides with another mask.

x1: [number] The position of the first mask horizontally within space.

y1: [number] The position of the first mask vertically within space.

angle1: [number] The rotation of the first mask.

mask2: [array] The second mask to check if it collides with the other mask.

x2: [number] The position of the second mask horizontally within space.

y2: [number] The position of the second mask vertically within space.

angle2: [number] The rotation of the second mask.
