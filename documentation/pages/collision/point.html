collision.point

collision.point(mask,mx,my,angle,px,py);

mask: [array] The mask to check for a collision.

mx: [number] The position of the mask horizontally within space.

my: [number] The position of the mask vertically within space.

angle: [number] Rotation of the mask.

px: [number] Vertical component of the point to see if it lies within the mask.

py: [number] Horizontal component of the point to see if it lies within the mask.
