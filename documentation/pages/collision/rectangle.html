collision.rectangle

collision.rectangle(mask,mx,my,angle,x1,y1,width,height);

mask: [array] The mask to check for a collision.

mx: [number] The position of the mask horizontally within space.

my: [number] The position of the mask vertically within space.

angle: [number] Rotation of the mask.

x1: [number] The horizontal position of the upper left point of the rectangle.

y1: [number] The vertical position of the upper left point of the rectangle.

width: [number] The width of the rectangle to check. Can be a negative number.

height: [number] The height of the rectangle to check. Can be a negative number.
